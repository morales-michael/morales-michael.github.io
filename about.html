The Analyst

Michael Morales

Currently pursuing a JD at UCLA Law, my work focuses on the intersection of NBA collective bargaining policy and front-office strategic execution.

This platform serves as a specialized resource for navigating the complexities of the NBA salary cap. By analyzing shifting market trends—such as the evolving utility of the Mid-Level Exception and the strategic benefits of declining contract structures—I aim to bridge the gap between legal mechanics and roster optimization.

Prior to my legal studies, I developed a deep interest in the financial infrastructure of professional sports, specifically how teams can leverage the CBA to create competitive advantages in the modern NBA economy.
